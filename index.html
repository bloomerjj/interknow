The Knotty Groom

The Knotty Groom is a traveling men’s stylist. Men are often left out of the market of Wedding Day self-care. The Knotty Groom focuses specifically on mens hair and facial hair styling for big events. IK was engaged to develop this edgy brand identity including logo and typography. We then applied the new graphic language to all marketing materials to better appeal to this unique clientele.
